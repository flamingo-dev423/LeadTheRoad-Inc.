Holiday Packages


This is our niche. Educational and Holiday Tour is our specialty. We know all the intricacies of educational travel and student holiday tour packages like the back of our hands. With all modesty, we conceive, design, package and organize the largest curriculum linked education tours with over 5,000 school students travelling through our travel network to different destinations across the world annually.

We basically infuse curriculum modules spiced with high dose of unlimited interactive and engaging games and fun in all our school trips, educational tours and holiday tour packages for school students. During every of our school trip, we ensure we link your school’s curriculum to all the places your students will be visiting which makes learning come alive.

In order to make the trip more engaging for your students, we create series of teambuilding and learning skills. We provide work-pack to each student to aid their learning skills during every tour. Hence, we say we Bring Learning to Life. We curate over 65 school holiday related and educational tour products which your school can choose from and will be customized specifically to suit your school’s tour requirements and travel goals. We are the only African company member of the Council for Learning Outside the Classroom (CLOtC) and we are a recipient of Quality Badge Award of the Council for Learning Outside the Classroom (CLOtC).
